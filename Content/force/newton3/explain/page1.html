Newton's Third Law

Preparation:Force/Newton's Third Law/Get A Glimpse.

Newton's Third Law states:

If an object A exerts a force on another object B, then object B also exerts a force on object A. The two forces have the same magnitude and opposite directions.

Click START to observe the motions of two objects resulting from Newton's Third Law. Use the toggle button SHOW BLOCKS/SHOW SKATERS to observe this for two different pairs of objects.
