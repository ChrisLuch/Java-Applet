Electric and Magnetic Forces

The magnetic field is now set to zero. There is a uniform electric field whose direction is in the plane of the screen. You can vary the electric field by dragging the tip of the E-vector. Do this and observe the resulting motion. Then PAUSE the motion and click the RESET button.

You can vary several more things:

  • the initial velocity (green arrow; drag the tip of the arrow)
  • the sign of the charge and the magnitude of the charge-to-mass ratio of the moving particle
  • the number of particles (1, 2, or 12; all particles are identical).
