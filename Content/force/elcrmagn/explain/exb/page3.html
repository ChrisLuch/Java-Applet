Electric and Magnetic Forces

Again, there is a uniform electric field in the plane of the screen.

Adjust the electric field vector so that it points straight down, and make sure the charge is positive. Adjust the initial velocity so that it points upward and to the right in a roughly 45-degree direction. Then click PLAY. You should be able to observe a motion that looks exactly like projectile motion of a massive particle in the earth's gravitational field.

You can pause the motion, repeat it, step through it, and display or hide the trace.

After clicking PLAY, always click PAUSE and then RESET if you want to change any of the parameters.
