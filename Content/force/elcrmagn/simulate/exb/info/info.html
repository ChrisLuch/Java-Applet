ExB Applet

Uniform electric field: indicated by the red arrow in the top left corner and present throughout the applet window. Drag the arrow at its tip to change the field.

Uniform magnetic field: perpendicular to the screen, either into the screen or out of the screen, and present throughout the applet window. Click the B-toggle button to reverse the field direction, and use the B-slider to change the magnitude of the field.

Initial velocity of the charged particle: represented by the green arrow. Vary it dragging the tip of the arrow .

Scale: used to make relative measurements of initial particle speed. Drag in the middle of the scale to move the scale, and drag either tip to change the orientation.

Number of particles: either 1, 2, or 12. A slider lets you change the number.

Sign of the charge and magnitude of the charge-to-mass ratio: change these with a toggle button or slider.

Particle tracks: turn on or off with a toggle button.

Play mode: either continuous or step; choose between the PLAY or STEP buttons.
