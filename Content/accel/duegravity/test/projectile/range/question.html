Projectile motion (conceptual)

A 3.0-Kg object is launched from the ground with some initial velocity at some angle above the horizontal, and falls back to the ground at a distance of 20 m from the launch point.  If a 1.0-kg object is thrown at the same angle with twice the initial speed of the first object, its range will be

  1. 40 m

  2. 60 m

  3. 80 m

  4. 120 m

  5. 240 m
