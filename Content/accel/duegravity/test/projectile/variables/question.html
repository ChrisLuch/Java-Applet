Projectile motion (conceptual)

A ball is thrown by a force of magnitude F = 30 N at an angle q = 30° above the the horizontal from a cliff of height h = 45 m.  The ball's initial velocity has a horizontal component v0x = 19 m/s and a vertical component v0y = 11 m/s.  Check the statement that is correct.

To determine the ball's range, which of the following is not useful?

  1. F

  2. v0x

  3. v0y

  4. h

  5. q.
