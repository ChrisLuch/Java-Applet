Cliff

Projectile motion (conceptual)

Three stunt drivers approach the edge of a cliff on parallel roads.  Driver 1 is going at 50 Km/h on a horizontal road, Driver 2 is going at 90 Km/h on another horizontal road, and Driver 3 is going at 90 Km/h while ascending on a road that is rising at an angle of 45° relative to the horizontal.  If all three drivers drive off the cliff's edge at the same time, the order in which they will reach the water 30 m below the cliff is:

  1. 1; 2; 3

  2. 2; 1; 3

  3. 1; then 2 and 3 simultaneously

  4. 1 and 2 simultaneously; then 3

  5. 2 and 3 simultaneously; then 1.
