A2: Activities on the Effect of the Launch Angle on Projectile Motion

In all of the following activities, keep the launch speed v(0) fixed and vary the launch angle q(0).

The "range" is the horizontal displacement of the projectile (ball) when it has fallen back down to the initial horizontal level, and the "time of flight" is the corresponding time elapsed.

The activities are in the form of multiple-choice statements. Use the applet to investigate which choices are correct, and try to explain your observations in terms of the theory of projectile motion. The equations describing projectile motion are summarized in "Equations" under Get Information. Close the present Activities window before accessing "Equations". Then close the Equations window before returning to the present Activities window.
