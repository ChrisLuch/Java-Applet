Four Motions

Click Play in any of the four quadrants on the left, and observe the motion of the object. You can run several motions at once, by clicking Play in several quadrants.

The Reset button in a quadrant allows you to reset the motion in that quadrant and play it again.

In some of the motions the acceleration is non-zero and in one or more of them it is zero at all times.

Question. In which of the four motions is the object's acceleration equal to zero?

Select (a), (b), (c), or (d) below, and then click SUBMIT to find out if the acceleration is zero or not for that motion.
