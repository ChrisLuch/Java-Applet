Energy Conservation in Fletcher's Trolley

Preparation: Energy/Energy Conserv'n/Explain It/Fletcher's Trolley.

The applet on Page 2 simulates a Fletcher's Trolley apparatus (a horizontally moving block connected via a pulley to a vertically moving block). The kinetic energies of the two blocks and of the pulley and the potential energy of the system are represented in bar form showing total energy conservation.

Detailed information on the applet and two lessons related to the applet are available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.
