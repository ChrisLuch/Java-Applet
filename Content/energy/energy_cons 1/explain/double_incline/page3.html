What is the Acceleration?

Now let us determine the acceleration theoretically, using energy conservation.

First, it is a good idea to become a bit familiar with the system. You can change the angles of the inclines (by dragging their 'feet') and the masses of the blocks. Do this for various settings and observe the accelerations.

Also observe the potential energies PE1 and PE2 of the pairs block-1/earth and block-2/earth, respectively.
