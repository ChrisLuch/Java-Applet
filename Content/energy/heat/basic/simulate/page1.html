Thermal Energy or Internal Energy

The applet on Page 2 simulates the conversion of work into internal energy and kinetic energy. The system involved consists of a block sliding on a table with friction. There is an applied force acting on the block that does external work on the system.

For details on how to use the applet, please go to Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.
