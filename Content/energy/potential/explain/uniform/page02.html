The Concept of Potential Energy

Answers. When the two bodies are farther apart, their potential energy is greater. When the distance between them decreases, so does the potential energy. You can observe this most easily by dragging one of the bodies before clicking PLAY.

Upon clicking PLAY, while the two bodies are moving subject to only their gravitational interaction, the system's kinetic energy increases as its potential energy decreases, and vice versa, so that the sum of the two energies remains constant.

Experiment. Let us observe this again, for the special case in which the two bodies move either directly away from or towards each other. To do so, adjust the initial velocities (red vectors) so that they either point towards or away from each other. Observe how, as the bodies 'rise' or 'fall', kinetic energy is converted into equal amounts of potential energy or vice versa, respectively. You can step through the motion if you like.
