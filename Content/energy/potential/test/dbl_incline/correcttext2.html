Which Way Will It Move?

Setting Up. The total potential energy PE is

PE = m1gh1 + m2gh2.

A z-direction is chosen to be up the incline on the left and down the incline on the right. Suppose both blocks displace by Dz, then their elevations change by

Dh1 = Dz sin a and Dh2 = -Dz sin b.

Solving. The change DPE of the potential energy is equal to

DPE = (m1 sin a - m2 sin b) g Dz

= (0.6 sin 30o - 0.4 sin 60o)x9.8x Dz = - 0.45 Dz.

If the left block moves up the incline, Dz > 0 and DPE < 0, which is what the system wants to do. Thus, if the blocks are released from rest, the left block will move up the incline and the right block down.
