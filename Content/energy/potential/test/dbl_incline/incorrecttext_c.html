You picked choice (c), saying that the blocks will remain at rest.

It is a bit hard to guess what will happen. Although the 0.6-kg block is heavier, the 0.4-kg block is on a steeper incline which compensates for its smaller weight.

The blocks will move so that their combined potential energy decreases as they move. They are in equilibrium if it does not change when they move.

Try to work out by how much the potential energy changes if both blocks move 1 m, in the same general direction, and see if this change is positive, negative or zero.
