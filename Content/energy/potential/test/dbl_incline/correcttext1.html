Which Way Will It Move?

Yes, the 0.6-kg block will move up, even though it is heavier than the other one. You can see this happening by clicking the START button. Observe the resulting change in total potential energy; as the masses move, the total length of the potential energy bars labeled PE1 and PE2 decreases.

Solution

Strategy. We will use the principle that a system of particles will try to lower its potential energy if allowed to move spontaneously.

The system consists of the earth and the two blocks. Its potential energy arises from the interactions between the earth and block 1 and the earth and block 2. (The interaction between the two blocks themselves is totally negligible.)

We will calculate the change in the system's total potential energy when the blocks undergo a given displacement and check if it is positive or negative. If negative, then this is the direction in which the blocks will move.
