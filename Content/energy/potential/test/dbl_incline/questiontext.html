Which Way Will It Move?

Two blocks on a frictionless double incline are connected via a string and a frictionless pulley. The masses of the blocks and the inclination angles are shown. You can drag either block and observe how the potential energy changes.

Answer the following question by using the principle that the blocks will move so that the combined potential energy decreases.

Upon release, which way will the left block move?

(a) It will move up.

(b) It will move down.

(c) It will remain at rest.
