Which Way Will It Move?

Thinking About the Answer. We found

DPE = (m1 sin a - m2 sin b) g Dz.

Thus, the direction of motion of the system will depend on the sign of the factor

(m1 sin a - m2 sin b) g.

In the present case, this factor is negative or

(-m1 sin a + m2 sin b) g > 0.

One gets the same condition by considering the components of the gravitational forces acting on the blocks in the direction of the inclines.

-m1 g sin a is the z-component of the gravitational force acting on block 1 and m2 g sin b that acting on block 2. If the sum of these two force components is positive, as it is in the present case, the blocks will accelerate in the positive z-direction. This confirms our answer.
