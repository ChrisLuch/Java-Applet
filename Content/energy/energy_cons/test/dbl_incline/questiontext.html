"What is the Acceleration?"

Two blocks on a frictionless double incline are connected via a string over a frictionless and massless pulley. The masses of the blocks and the inclination angles are shown.

Use energy conservation to calculate the blocks' acceleration. You can drag the blocks and observe how the potential energy changes.

Take the acceleration as positive if the left block accelerates up the incline, negative if it accelerates down the incline.
