What is the Acceleration?

Setting up

The total kinetic energy, KE, is equal to

KE = (m1/2) v2 + (m2/2) v2 = (M/2) v2

where M = m1 + m2 is the combined mass of the blocks.

The total potential energy, PE, is equal to

PE = m1gh1 + m2gh2.

Thus, the total mechanical energy of the system, E, is equal to

E = KE + PE = (M/2) v2 + m1gh1 + m2gh2.

The total energy E does not change as the blocks accelerate and v, h1, and h2 change. This implies the following relation between the changes of v, h1, and h2:

DE = 0 = (M/2)Dv2 + m1gDh1 + m2gDh2

whence

Dv2 = -2 [ (m1/M) g Dh1 + (m2/M) g Dh2 ].
