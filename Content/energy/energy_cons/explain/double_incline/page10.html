What is the Acceleration?

The equation for az can be rewritten

Maz = -m1g sin a + m2g sin b .

This means that the two blocks act like a single block of mass M moving along a z-axis while acted upon by the sum of the z-components of the gravitational forces acting on the blocks.

So far, we have assumed the pulley mass to be negligible. How does the equation above have to be modified if the pulley's mass is not zero?

You can now set the pulley mass to a non-zero value and observe the acceleration. Can you predict theoretically what you observe? Try to calculate the acceleration. The next page will show you how.
