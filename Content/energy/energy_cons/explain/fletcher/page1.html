Energy Conservation in Fletcher's Trolley

Preparation: Energy/Potential Energy/Explain It/Uniform Force.

The Fletcher Trolley apparatus shown on the left is frictionless. Therefore the mechanical energy of the system consisting of this apparatus and the earth is conserved. The mechanical energy is the sum of the kinetic energies of all moving parts and the gravitational potential energy. As always, the earth's kinetic energy can be ignored.

Question. Drag block 1 to the left until block 2 is raised to a height of h = 2.5 m. Then run the simulation until block 1 reaches h = 0. Can you predict, using energy conservation, what the speed of the two blocks will be at this moment? Try it.
