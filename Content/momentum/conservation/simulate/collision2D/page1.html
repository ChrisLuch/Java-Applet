Collisions In Two Dimensions

The applet on Page 2 simulates a collision between an incoming object and an object that is initially stationary. The impact parameter of the incoming object can be set to a non-zero value which results in both objects moving away from the collision axis after the collision. The collision can be viewed both in the laboratory frame of reference and the center-of-mass frame of reference.

The Help, ShowMe, and Lesson documents available under Related Items have the following functions:

Help - Explanation of the applet's functionality in point form.

ShowMe - Demonstration of the applet's features in the form of exercises.

Lesson - Lessons on qualitative and quantitative aspects related the applet.
