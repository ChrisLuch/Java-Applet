Instantaneous Velocity

Useful Observation About Velocity

The velocity of an object points in the direction in which the tip of the object's position vector is moving.

Use the simulation to observe this as follows. Uncheck everything except 'Velocity Vector' and 'Position Vector'. Produce a motion with the velocity dial. Then click RESTART and rerun the motion in continuous mode by clicking PLAY or by stepping through it using the STEP button. As you watch the motion, think about the statement above.

Display the car's path by checking 'Path' and observe that the velocity vector points in the direction of the tangent to the path at any given instant. This is the direction in which the tip of the position vector is moving at that instant.

You may want to view the motion in the car's frame by checking 'Car's Frame'.
