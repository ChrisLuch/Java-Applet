Simulation of Velocity vs. Position

Preparation: Velocity/Basic Facts/Explain It.

The applet is on Page 2. Instructions on how to use it will become available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu. Currently, no Help is available.

The applet lets you control the motion of a car by means of a velocity dial, i.e., by controlling the car's velocity. The purpose of the simulation is to demonstrate the relationship between the car's position and velocity.
