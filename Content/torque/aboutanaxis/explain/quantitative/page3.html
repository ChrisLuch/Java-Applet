Calculating a Torque

In applications, the value of the lever arm is often not given but can be calculated. Suppose that given are:

  • the distance r (shown in blue) between point O and the point A at which the force is acting,
  • the angle q between the force and the direction from O to A.

Task: Calculate the lever arm for the given values of r and q. Click CHECK VALUE to compare results.

For help with the geometry, click Hint.

You can repeat the exercise, for a different configuration, by moving the force elsewhere (dragging the midpoint of the vector), by changing the force (dragging its tip), or by changing the reference point (dragging it).
