Calculating a Torque

With Fperp = F sin q, we can rewrite the expression for the torque as

t = (sign) Fl = (sign) F (r sin q)

= (sign) r (F sin q)

= (sign) rFperp .

Use the last expression for the torque and the values for r and Fperp to calculate the torque relative to O. Click CHECK VALUE to compare values.
