The Lever Arm

You can see a force vector and a rotation axis. The following sequence will take you through the construction of the lever arm for this situation. The lever arm depends on both the force vector and where it acts in relation to the axis.

Step 1. Draw the line along which the force is acting. To do this, click STEP 1.

Step 2. Drop the perpendicular from the axis onto the line along which the force is acting. To do this, click STEP 2. The perpendicular, in green, is the lever arm.

To repeat the sequence with a different force, click NEW FORCE.
