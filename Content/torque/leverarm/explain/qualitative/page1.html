The Lever Arm

The diagram shows a force vector, in mustard color, and a rotation axis perpendicular to the screen through point O, in red. The point at which the force is acting, in blue, is at the tail end of the force vector. The corresponding lever arm is shown in green. The dotted line is the 'line along which the force is acting'.

You can vary the following by dragging:

  • position of rotation axis
  • force vector (drag its tip)
  • point at which force is applied.

Observe how the lever arm changes.
