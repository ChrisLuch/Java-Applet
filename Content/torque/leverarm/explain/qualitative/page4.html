The Lever Arm

Point B is in exactly the same position as on p.3, where the beam was balanced. However, now the 200-N force is not acting vertically, but at an angle. This changes the lever arm, and therefore the torque due to the 200-N force. The torque due to the ball is unchanged. As a result, the beam is no longer balanced.
