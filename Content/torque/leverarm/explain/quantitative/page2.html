Calculating a Lever Arm

Suppose that you are given

  • the distance r between O and A and
  • the angle q between the force and the direction from O to A.

Task: Calculate the length of the lever arm. Click CHECK VALUE to compare results.

For help with the geometry, click Hint.

Repeat the exercise for a different configuration. This may be more of a challenge than you think. The lever arm may be configured quite differently when the force vector and the point where it is acting are changed.
