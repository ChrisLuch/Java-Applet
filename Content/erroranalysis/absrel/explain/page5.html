Combining Types of Error

The applet on the left will take you through a series of three questions. After you have answered the first one correctly, the next one will be presented.

The questions deal with both absolute and relative error. You'll be asked to calculate both, and something a little bit different in Question 3.

Remember the equation

rel. err. = (abs. err.) / (best estimate) = DL / L

where in the symbolic expression on the right it has been assumed that the quantity to be measured has the symbol L.

From this equation, you can express any of the three quantities occurring in it in terms of the other two. The three questions ask you, in turn, for each of the three quantities given the other two.
