An explaination of how to find the components of a vector is available when you select 'Demo' from the Mode menu. You may also want to read up on vector components in the reference item.

Determine the x,y component of the vector to one decimal accuracy and type your answer in the space provided below. To help you find the components, you can drag the vector (by clicking on it and moving the mouse with its button depressed) and you can draw dotted lines on the screen. To delete a line, select it by clicking on it and then click the Delete Line button.
