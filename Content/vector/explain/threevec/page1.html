The following demonstration will show how to add three vectors using the tip-to-tail method.

This requires that you know how to add two vectors by this method. For a review, go to the Preparation above.

To practise adding three vectors, with your own choice of vectors, go to Vectors/Addition/Simulate It/Three Vectors.
