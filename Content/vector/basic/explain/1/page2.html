Vectors
Basic Facts

The direction of a vector can be measured by the angle between the vector and the 'eastward' direction (dotted line).

Drag the tip of the vector. (Click the tip of the vector, and move the mouse with the mouse button depressed.) Investigate for what directions the angle is positive or negative.

The angle could also be defined differently from the way it is done here. E.g., it could be defined so that it is positive all the way around, increasing from 0 to 360o as the vector rotates counter-clockwise.
