Addition of Three Vectors

Three vectors are drawn on the screen and labeled.

Task: In your notebook, draw the three vectors and try to find their sum.

Do this by joining the three vectors tip-to-tail: first the tail end of the second vector to the tip of the first one, then the tail of the third vector to the tip of the second one.

It does not matter which vectors you take to be the first, second, and third vectors.

When done, go on to the next page to compare.
