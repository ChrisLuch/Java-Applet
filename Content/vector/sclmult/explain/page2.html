Multiplication of a Vector by a Scalar

In the simulation on the left, choose the vector you want to multiply by a scalar.

You can choose your vector by dragging the tip of the yellow arrow (by clicking on it and moving the mouse with the mouse button depressed).

On the next page you will be able to graphically multiply the vector by a scalar.

Question. What vector will you get when you multiply the vector by 2, or -1.5? Try to draw the resulting vectors on a sheet of paper.
