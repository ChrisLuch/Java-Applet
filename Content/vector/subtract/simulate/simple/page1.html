Subtraction Of Vectors

Preparation:

  • Vectors/Subtraction/Explain It/Add-the-Negative
  • Vectors/Subtraction/Explain It/Comparison

The applet on Page 2 will let you

  • draw two vectors,
  • find their difference graphically, either by the Add-the-Negative method or the Comparison method,
  • compare the difference as drawn by you to the correct difference.
Details on how to use the applet and a lesson on vector subtraction, including related calculations, are available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.
