Scalar Components of Vectors

The diagram shows the scalar components of a vector , the x-component in green and the y-component in yellow.

If the tail end of the vector is at the origin, its scalar components are simply the x and y coordinates of its tip, here equal to -4 and 6, respectively.

The scalar components of vector are scalars, denoted ax and ay.

You can denote vector in terms of its scalar components as follows:

= (ax, ay) = (-4, 6).
