Scalar Components of Vectors

Now rotate the axes by clicking the tip of one of the axes and dragging it. You will not be able to change the vector.

Observe that the components change while the axes are being rotated, but that the vector itself does not change.

Therefore, in specifying a vector by its components, you must specify the axes relative to which the components are taken.

Giving just the components is incomplete information.

Try to adjust the axes so that the vector has the components ax = 4 and ay = -6, or ax = 6.5 and ay = 3.1 (or close to it). Can you adjust the axes so that the coordinates have any values whatsoever?
