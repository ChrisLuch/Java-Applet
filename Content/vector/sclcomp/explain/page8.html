Scalar Components of Vectors

Answer. Applying the Pythagorean theorem to either one of the two right-angle triangles in the applet that have vector as their hypotenuse gives for the magnitude a of vector the expression

a = (ax2 + ay2)

= [(-3.0)2 + 4.02]

= 5.0.

For angle q we have the equation (see Trig Functions under Get Information)

tan q = ay/ax

whence

q = tan-1 ay/ax

= tan-1 4.0/(-3.0) = 127o.
