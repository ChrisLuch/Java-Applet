To draw a vector or a line or dotted line, select the appropriate tool button and then draw with the mouse. To delete an item, select it (by clicking on it) and then press DELETE.

When you have drawn two component vectors on the screen, the program will automatically display their sum in green if it equals within a reasonable margin, and if the two component vectors are reasonably orthogonal to each other. Otherwise the sum will be shown in red. The angle between your two component vectors is indicated at the bottom. Your task is to construct two vectors such that this angle is 90 degrees and the resultant vector turns green.

You can drag any of the vectors or lines on the screen, except for the vector in red (or green). You can change any of the vectors or lines that you have drawn by clicking on them at one of their endpoints and then dragging this endpoint.
