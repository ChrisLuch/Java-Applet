Vector Components

Three different pairs of orthogonal vectors 1 and 2 are shown, joined tip-to-tail each time.

In each case, 1 + 2 is equal to the same vector and the vectors 1 and 2 are perpendicular to each other.

Thus all three pairs 1 and 2 are Cartesian vector components of the same vector .
