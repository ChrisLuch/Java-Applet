Vector Components

The applet on the left will demonstrate how to construct a pair of Cartesian vector components for an arbitrary vector along an arbitrary pair of orthogonal x,y axes.

You can change the vector shown now by dragging its tip.

When you have the vector you want, click "Draw Axes" to have the applet draw two orthogonal x,y axes through the tail end of . You can rotate these axes by dragging the tip of either axis.

Then click "Draw Perps" to have the applet drop perpendiculars from the tip of onto the two axes.

Then click "Draw Comps" to have the applet draw the vector components x and y

The vector components point from the tail end of , at the origin of the axes, to where the perpendiculars meet the axes.

Click the Reset button, and repeat the construction for another vector and another pair of x,y axes.
