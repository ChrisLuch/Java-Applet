Position Vector

Arrange things so that only the ball and the reference point are visible.

Then click SHOW AXES. A pair of x and y axes will be shown with their origin at the reference point. Observe the values of the x and y coordinates of the ball's position printed out by the program.

Move the ball somewhere else and observe how its position coordinates change.

Now click SHOW POSITION VECTOR. The program prints out the x and y scalar components of this vector, in parentheses. The lines shown in yellow on the axes represent these components.

See Scalar Components under Get Information.
