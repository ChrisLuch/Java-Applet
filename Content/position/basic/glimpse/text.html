Position

Physicists tends to simplify things, sometimes to the point of caricature. In so doing, they are creating models of nature that are easier to deal with than the full reality.

For example, a bee like the one in the picture on the left gets treated like a mass point of zero extent, and one then talks about the position of the bee.

Sometimes one has to do better than that and treat the bee as an extended object, whose parts have their own positions. In such a case, it is useful to consider the center of mass of the bee, which again is a single point with a single position, and to use this as the "position of the bee".
