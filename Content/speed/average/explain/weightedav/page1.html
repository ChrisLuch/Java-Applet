Time-weighted Average of Speeds

Preparation: Speed/Average/Explain It/(Distance/Time)

The explanation of average speed on the following pages uses the applet on the left. For instructions on how to use the applet see Help and ShowMe under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu. In addition, it is recommended you work through the Lesson under Applet Help.

You may want to experiment a bit with the applet before going on to Page 2.

Exercise. Play the applet and vary the speed by means of the slider. The speed is plotted vs. time in red. At the same time the corresponding average speed calculated from the initial moment t = 0 to the current instant of time t is plotted in blue. For details see the Help and ShowMe files.

Hide the speed vs. time graph by deselecting its checkbox, and observe the object whose speed is being plotted.
