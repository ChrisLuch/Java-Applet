Time-weighted Average of Speeds

Exercise. RESET the applet, and hide the speed vs. time graph so that the car is visible.

Set the speed to 12.0 m/s, and PLAY the motion. PAUSE it when the car is half-way around its oval orbit (or close to it). Change the speed to 24.0 m/s, and continue the motion until the car has completed one round (or close to it).

Calculate the car's average speed for one round.

Display the speed vs. time graph. Compare the exact value of the average speed displayed in blue with your calculated value.
