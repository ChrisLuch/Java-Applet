Average Speed

The ball's average speed, vav, over a given time interval is defined as the ratio (distance traveled) / (time elapsed); in symbols:

vav = s/t.

Here s denotes the distance traveled, and t is equal to the time elapsed.

Note that, in general, the time elapsed in the denominator should be denoted Δt, not t. However, since in this case the time interval in the applet starts at t = 0, Δt = t - 0 = t.

Substitute the values of the distance s traveled by the ball and the time t elapsed into the definition above to calculate the average speed for the motion. Compare your result to the value of vav in the Data box.
