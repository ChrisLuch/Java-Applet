Kirchhoff's Laws

Kirchhoff's Loop Rule applied to the clockwise loop from B to C to B gives

-R3I3 - R2I2 = 0.

The two minus signs come about because in going across both resistors R3 and R2 we are going in positive branch directions, i.e., 'downhill' if the currents actually are in these directions.

The other two equations, derived earlier, are listed once more below:

-R1I1 + R2I2 + 12 V = 0

I1 + I2 = I3.

Try to solve these three equations for the three unknowns I1 to I3. Use the R-values from the circuit or work with symbols. Then go on to Page 7 to compare.
