Several Batteries

The three equations

I1 + I2 + I3 = 0

-4I1 + 2.0 + 2I2 - 6.0 = 0

6.0 - 2I2 + 6I3 + 4.0 = 0 .

have the solutions

I1 = -3/11 A

I2 = 16/11 A

I3 = -13/11 A .

You can verify these values by substituting them into the equations above. You can also check them 'experimentally' by inserting ammeters into the three branches.

Currents 1 and 3 are downward and current 2 is upward. This means that battery 1 is charging and batteries 2 and 3 are discharging. (Note the battery polarities.)
