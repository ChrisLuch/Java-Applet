Electric Circuits

The animation shows the hydrologic cycle. Electric circuits are in several basic ways like the hydrologic cycle.

First of all, both are circuits. Can you explain what is the essential characteristic of a circuit?

In addition, in both the hydrologic cycle and electric circuits two fundamental conservation laws are realized. Can you think of which ones? (The two conservation laws realized in the two kinds of circuits are not the same, but analogous.)
