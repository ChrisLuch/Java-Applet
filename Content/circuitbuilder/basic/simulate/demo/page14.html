Other Controls

There is one more useful attribute of the Select Tool that you need to know about. Select the Select Tool and then click on a wire segment. At the bottom of the display about this segment, there are three buttons, marked with either arrows or a dash, respectively. Click on one of the two arrow buttons. This will let you choose a direction in the branch of the circuit containing the wire segment. This will be useful in applying Kirchhoff's laws when solving circuit problems. Clicking the button with the dash will deselect the direction.

The only remaining tools are the Zoom-In Tool  and the Zoom-Out Tool  .  Click on these to see what effect they have on the display.

All tools are now active, and you may want to experiment freely with the simulation before quitting this demo. You can do so also by going to Electrical Circuits/Basic Facts/Simulate It/Practice. There you will have the entire window area available for the Circuit Lab Simulation.

If you point the mouse to one of the tool icons, a tool tip will appear indicating the function of the tool.
