Batteries

Select the Select Tool .  Then click on the battery in the circuit.  Information about the battery will be displayed, including its emf and its internal resistance.

Their values are characteristic of the battery and do not depend on anything else in the circuit.  Change them via the data entry fields. When done, click the Calculator icon to implement the new values. Observe how the other values on display change.

The other listed quantities, the current in the battery, the voltage across the external terminals of the battery, and the power of conversion of chemical to electrical energy in the battery, are not properties of the battery alone, but depend also on the other circuit elements.

You can change the polarity of the battery so that the positive pole is on the right rather than on the left by clicking the "Switch Polarity" button.  Try it, and observe how the current direction changes.
