Resistors

To get rid of the short circuit, insert a resistor into the circuit.

To do so, select the resistor tool (circuit symbol: ) by clicking on it.  Then click on the wire segment into which you want to insert the resistor.

When you have done this correctly, the short circuit will cease to exist, as indicated by black rather than red wires.

On the next page, you will be shown how to set the resistance of the resistor to a specific value.
