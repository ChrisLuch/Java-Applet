Electric Current

The picture shows the essential elements of a light bulb. The picture shows the bulb cut open at the bottom so that you can see how the filament is connected to the outside metal contacts.

The filament is the part that gets heated by the current and emits the light. One end of the filament is connected to the metal thread by which the bulb is screwed into the socket. The other end is connected to a metal contact at the base of the bulb.

To light the bulb, its metal thread must be connected to one pole of the battery and the metal contact at the base to the other pole.
