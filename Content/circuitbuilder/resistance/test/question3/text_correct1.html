The resistance of each one of the three resistors is equal to 2 W.

Solution

Plan It. The value of the total resistance Rtot of the combination of the three resistors can be calculated from the values of the battery terminal potential difference and the current in the circuit.

This value can be expressed in terms of R, the resistance of one of the resistors.
