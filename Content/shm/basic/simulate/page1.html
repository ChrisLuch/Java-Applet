Simple Harmonic Motion

The applet on Page 2 simulates simple harmonic motion (shm) as performed by a weight suspended from a spring. It is demonstrated that this motion is simple harmonic motion by showing that the motion is projected uniform circular motion.

Detailed information on how to use the applet and a lesson on shm is available from the applet's Help menu.

The applet allows variation of

  • spring constant,
  • mass,
  • amplitude,
  • initial phase.
