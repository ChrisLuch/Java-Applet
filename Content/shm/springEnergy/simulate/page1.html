Energy Conservation in the Simple Harmonic Motion of a Weighted Spring

The applet on Page 2 simulates energy conservation in the simple harmonic motion performed by a weight suspended from a spring.

Detailed information on how to use the applet and a lesson on shm with exercises using the applet are available from the applet's Help menu.

The applet allows variation of

  • spring constant,
  • mass,
  • amplitude,
  • initial phase.
