Energy Conservation in the Simple Harmonic Motion of a Pendulum

The applet on Page 2 simulates energy conservation in the simple harmonic motion (approximately) performed by a simple pendulum.

Detailed information on how to use the applet and a lesson containing exercises to be done with the applet are available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.

The applet allows variation of the following parameters:

  • length of pendulum,
  • mass of pendulum bob,
  • acceleration due to gravity,
  • amplitude,
  • initial phase.
