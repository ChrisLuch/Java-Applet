Hooke's Law Videolab

This experiment investigates the dynamic properties of a spring by testing Hooke's Law.

The equipment consists of a simple spring, various masses, and a stopwatch. The mass on the spring is varied from 200 to 500 g, and the time for 10 oscillations is measured.
