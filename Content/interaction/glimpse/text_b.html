(b) Projectile Motion

The green bar at the bottom of the applet window is supposed to represent the surface of the earth. When you PLAY the motion, the red particle performs a parabolic motion.

This kind of motion is called projectile motion. If you throw a ball into the air, it will perform approximately the kind of motion simulated here. (Not exactly, because a real ball experiences air resistance while the red particle in the applet does not.)

In the applet, the red particle is interacting gravitationally with the entire earth. If the earth were not there, the red particle's motion would be quite different.
