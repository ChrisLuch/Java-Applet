Timer Stopwatch

The applet is on Page 2. Details on how to use it are available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.

The applet simulates both a stopwatch and a timer, with minimal delay between clicking the Start/Stop button and the response of the clock.

In the Forward mode, the clock runs forward and simulates a stopwatch. In the Backward mode, the clock runs backward and simulates a timer.
