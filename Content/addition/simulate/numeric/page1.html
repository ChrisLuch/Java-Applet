Addition of Vecotrs Specified Numerically

Preparation:
1. Vectors/Basic Facts/Explain It/Vector Specification
2. Vectors/Addition/Explain It/

The applet will add two or more vectors, which you can specify numerically, and will display the resultant, both graphically and numerically.

You have a choice of four different numerical formats in specifying vectors and displaying the resultant of the vectors entered.

Details on how to use the applet are given under Help next to the MAP logo.
