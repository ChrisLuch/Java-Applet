Vector Addition Practice

Preparation: Vectors/Addition/Explain It/Tip-to-Tail and Vectors/Addition/Explain It/Parallelogram.

Draw two vectors in the drawing area. They will be labeled for you. You can change the vectors as much as you like (by dragging their tips or tail ends).

The simulation will let you practise adding these vectors by the tip-to-tail and the parallelogram methods.
