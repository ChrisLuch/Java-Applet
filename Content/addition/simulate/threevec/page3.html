Three Vectors

Draw the sum of the three vectors.

To draw a vector, first click on the button with the vector icon, then draw the vector. To delete a vector or dotted line, select the vector line by clicking on it. Then click the DELETE button.

You can use either the tip-to-tail method or the parallelogram method to construct the sum.
