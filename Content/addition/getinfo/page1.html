Vector Addition

There exist two graphical methods for obtaining the sum of two vectors:

(a) the tip-to-tail method and

(b) the parallelogram method.

These will be explained on the following pages.

For information on how to calculate the magnitude and direction of the sum of two vectors working with magnitudes and direction angles defining the two vectors, please see Vectors/Adddition/Explain It/Add'n Quantitative.

For information on how to calculate the sum of two vectors using scalar components, please see Vectors/Scalar Components/Explain It.
