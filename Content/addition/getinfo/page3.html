Vector Addition
Parallelogram Method

 Again, we want to add the two vectors and shown on the left, this time by using the parallelogram method.

In this method we join the vectors tail-to-tail, not tip-to-tail. The two vectors then span a parallelogram, as shown below.

A parallelogram has two diagonals. The one that points from the common tail end to the opposite corner represents the sum of the two vectors, shown in green.

Suppose the vectors and are force vectors. Then + is a single force that acting by itself has the same effect as the two individual forces and acting at the same time.
