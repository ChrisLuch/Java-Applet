Assumptions - Magnetic Field Due To Current In One Or Several Circular Loops
The applet simulates the magnetic field due to current in a circular wire loop or due to current in several such loops arranged coaxially.

The applet makes the following assumptions:

  • All loops carry the same current.
  • The loops are coaxial and circular with the same radius a.
  • The spacing between two neighboring loops is the same for any two such loops.
  • The axis through the centers of the loops lies in the screen and is horizontal. The loops lie in planes perpendicular to the screen.

As a consequence of the positioning and orientation of the loops, the magnetic field vector at a point in the screen lies in the screen also.
