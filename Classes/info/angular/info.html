Angular Velocity


Click on a point to view it's explanation

Explanation of Points

  1. Graph
    This graph represents the relationship between angular velocity and time.  Hold down the Ctrl key to adjust its origin, or simply click and drag the graph anywhere to adjust its location.
  2. Reference Point
    Click and drag this point to see how it affects the angular velocity.
  3. Play/Pause
    This button will start and pause the execution of the experiment.
  4. Step
    Press this button once to step through one iteration of the experiment.  The amount of time of the step is determined by the Step slider.
  5. Reset
    Pressing this button will reset the experiment.  The reference point will return to its original position as will the car.
  6. Replay
    Press this button to replay the experiment using the current settings.  Only the car's position will be reset but the reference point will remain where it was last positioned.
  7. Help
    Press this button to bring up this help page.
  8. Trajectory
    Choose among three types of trajectory:
    Line - This shows a trajectory of a straight line.
    Circle - This shows a circular trajectory.
    Arc - This shows a parabolic trajectory.
  9. Direction
    Choose a direction for the object in the simulation to travel.
  10. Step Time
    This slider determines how much time is elapsed when the Step button is pressed.
  11. Speed
    This slider determines the speed of the object in the experiment.
