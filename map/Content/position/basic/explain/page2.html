Position

Display the ball's position vector, and click the Axes button. A pair of x and y axes will be shown with their origin at the reference point O.

The values of the x and y coordinates of the ball's position are displayed. These coordinates are also the scalar components of the position vector. (For a review of scalar components, go to Scalar Components under Related Items.) The scalar components of the ball's position vector are indicated on the axes as pale blue lines.

In the third line in the data column in the top right corner of the applet window, the components of the position vector are displayed in the form (..., ...) m. The first entry in parentheses is the x-component, the second one the y-component. They are identical with the x and y coordinates of the ball's position.

For this reason, one denotes the x and y components of the position vector by x and y, not rx and ry.
