Position Vector

Click on the ball and drag it, or click anywhere in the screen area, to place the ball in a new position.

There is a reference point labeled O. You can move the reference point by clicking on it and dragging.

Place the ball in relation to the reference point, e.g., above O. The position of the ball can be described by the displacement vector from O to the ball. This vector is called the position vector of the ball.
