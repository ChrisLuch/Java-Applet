Acceleration in Fletcher's Trolley

We want to solve the two equations

T = m1a.(1)

and

W2 - T = m2a.(2)

When we add the two left-hand sides and the two right-hand sides, the tension cancels and we get an equation that involves only the unknown a,

W2 = (m1 + m2)a(4)

whence

a = W2/(m1 + m2)

= [m2/(m1 + m2)] g(5)

where W2 = m2g has been used.

Substituting into equation (5) the given values we obtain

a = [0.4/(0.6 + 0.4)] x 9.8

= 3.92 m s-2.(6)
