Block on Horizontal Surface with Friction

The applet on Page 2 simulates the motion of a block on a horizontal surface. The block is subject to friction and to an applied force. Both static and kinetic friction are simulated.

For detailed information on how to use the applet, please see Help, Assumptions, and ShowMe under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.

For suggestions on what to investigate with the applet, see Activities under Related Items.
