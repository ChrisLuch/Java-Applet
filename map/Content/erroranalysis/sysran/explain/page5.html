Random Error

On the left is an applet that tests your ability to "count" a length of time in your head.

First, the applet will demonstrate how to count time. An amount of time is indicated in yellow inside a yellow rectangular border. Click the "Demonstrate" button to see a count-down of this time.

Then, if you click the "Go on your own" button, you're on your own for ten trials. A target time will be displayed, and there will be a "Start" button. Click the "Start" button, and the invisible clock will be running. Click the button again - it will have turned into a "Stop" button - when you think the target time has elapsed.

After each trial, you will be shown the "target" time and "your" time... you might need to make some adjustments.

After the ten trials, click a "View Results" button to have your results displayed in a bar graph. Good luck.
