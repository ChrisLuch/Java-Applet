Simple Harmonic Motion of a Simple Pendulum

The applet on Page 2 simulates the oscillatory motion performed by a simple pendulum and shows that this motion is (approximately) the same as projected uniform circular motion and therefore (approximately) simple harmonic motion.

Detailed information on how to use the applet and a lesson on shm is available under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu.

The applet allows variation of the following parameters:

  • length of pendulum,
  • mass of pendulum bob,
  • acceleration due to gravity,
  • amplitude,
  • initial phase.
