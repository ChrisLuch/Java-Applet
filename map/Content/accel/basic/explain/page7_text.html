Acceleration

Question. What would the motion of the car whose accelerations and velocities were simulated on Pages 5 and 6 look like in Position Space? You can observe this motion by checking and unchecking appropriate checkboxes:

  • uncheck Velocity Space
  • check Pos Path
  • uncheck Vel Path
  • uncheck Accel #2
  • check Position Vector

Drag the object to a place in the applet window so that as much as possible of the object's blue position path is visible in the window. Click Replay and then Play. You may want to view the motion with the Car checkbox unchecked.

Exercise. Reset the applet and make the checkbox changes indicated above, but uncheck Position Vector. In addition, uncheck the Car checkbox, and check Accel #1. Make the mass point perform projectile motion.

Hint: Drag the mass point in the lower left-hand corner of the screen, set the initial velocity to point upward and to the right, and then choose a constant acceleration vector that points straight down.

Observe the way the velocity changes during the motion.

Replay the motion and observe that the acceleration either points to the inside of the mass point's trajectory or along the tangent to the trajectory, but never away from the trajectory to the outside of the trajectory. Why not?

Try this with other motions besides projectile motion.
