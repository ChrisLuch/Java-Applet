Books

Conceptual questions on free fall and motion on an incline without friction.

The Mad Doctor Kaeding has finally lost it.  He has climbed to the top of the Physical Sciences Building and is throwing physics books from the roof.  Shouts from the crowd include "Jump!", "Viva Gorditas!" and "Ignore Air Resistance!"

He drops the first book but throws the second one downward at 2.0 m/s.  Which statement that is correct.

After release of the books,

  1. The first book has greater acceleration than the second one.

  2. The second book has greater acceleration than the first one.

  3. Both have accelerations of the same magnitude, but in opposite directions.

  4. Both have accelerations of the same magnitude in the same direction.
