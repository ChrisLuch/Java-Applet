Ocean

Projectile Motion (conceptual)

Ball A is dropped from the edge of a cliff that overlooks the ocean.  Ball B is launched horizontally at the same moment from the edge of the same cliff.  If air resistance is ignored,

  1. ball A reaches the water first

  2. ball B reaches the water first

  3. ball A has the greater speed when it reaches the water

  4. ball B has the greater speed when it reaches the water

  5. ball A and B reach the water with the same speed.
