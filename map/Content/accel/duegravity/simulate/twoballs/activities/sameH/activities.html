ACTIVITIES FOR THE TWO-BALL APPLET

ACTIVITY 4: TWO BALLS LAUNCHED HORIZONTALLY FROM THE SAME HEIGHT BUT DIFFERENT HORIZONTAL POSITIONS AND WITH DIFFERENT SPEEDS

Set positions and velocity components for both balls as follows:

Ball 1: (x(0),y(0)) = (0,50) m, v1x(0) = ?, v1y (0) = 0

Ball 2: (x(0),y(0)) = (20,50) m, v2x (0) = 20.0 m/s, v2y (0) = 0

Make sure the applet is set to negligible air resistance and that the masses of the balls are the same. (The mass of Ball 1 is preset by the applet to 0.1 kg. The mass of Ball 2 can be varied. Make sure it is set to 0.1 kg.)

Which of the following values for v1x(0) will ensure that both balls will collide upon reaching the x-axis?

(a) 20.0 m/s
(b) 26.3 m/s
(c) 40.0 m/s

Set v1x(0) to your selected value. Launch the balls and see if the balls meet as you would expect. If they don't, revise your selection for v1x(0) and try again.
