Four Motions

Click in any of the four quadrants on the left and observe the motion of the object. You can run several motions at once, by clicking in several quadrants.

If you click a second time in one quadrant, the simulation in that quadrant will be reset and you can view the motion again.

In three of the four motions the object is accelerating. There is only one motion in which the acceleration is zero. Which motion is that?

Select (a), (b), (c), or (d) below and then click SUBMIT to find out.
