(b) Projectile Motion

To start the motion, click anywhere in the simulation window. To reset, click a second time.

This motion is called projectile motion. If you throw a ball into the air, it will perform approximately the kind of motion that is simulated here. (Not exactly, because of air resistance.)

The object performing projectile motion is interacting with the entire earth while performing this motion. This is a gravitational interaction.

The surface of the earth is shown in green at the bottom of the simulation window.
