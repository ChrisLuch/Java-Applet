Torque and Rotation

The simulation is now set up so that a single downward force is acting at one corner of the rectangular object. The initial velocity of the object is upward and to the right. PLAY the simulation and observe the motion. You can interrupt the motion by clicking the PLAY/PAUSE button and then resume the motion by clicking the button again.

The object performs two motions at once:

  • a translational motion (parabolic) of the center of mass (CM)
  • a rotational motion, which is oscillatory in this case, around the CM.
