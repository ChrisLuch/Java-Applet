The Lever Arm

A non-zero force will have zero torque about an axis if its lever arm is zero. You can use the simulation on the left to find siutations in which the lever arm is zero.

Click either the up or down arrows next to ROTATE FORCE or ROTATE POINT.

ROTATE FORCE will cause the force vector to change in direction. ROTATE POINT will cause the point at which the force is acting to move in a circle around the rotation axis.

There are many arrrangements in which the lever arm is zero. Try to find some of them.
