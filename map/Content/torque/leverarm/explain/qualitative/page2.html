The Lever Arm

The beam can rotate about point O. A 100-kg iron ball is suspended from the beam. Where must the upward 200-N force act so that it will lift the ball?

This is a matter of adjusting the lever arm, shown in green. To adjust the lever arm, drag point B at which the force is acting either right or left.

Upon clicking RELEASE, the system will be released and you can see if the 200-N force has sufficient torque to lift the ball. To try another position, click RESET.
