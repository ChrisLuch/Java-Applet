Calculating a Torque

Perpendicular Force Component Method

The diagram on the left shows a force acting at a point A and the force component perpendicular to the line OA, Fperp. One can calculate Fperp from the magnitude F of the force and the angle q between the direction OA and the direction of the force by

Fperp = F sin q' = F sin q.

Calculate Fperp from the given values of F and q to calculate Fperp. Click CHECK VALUE to compare results. On the next page you can see how Fperp can be used to calculate the torque.
