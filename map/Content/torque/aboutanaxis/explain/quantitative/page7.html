Calculating a Torque

F-q-r Method

The equations on Page 6 show that the calculation of the torque amounts to calculating the product

t = (sign) F (sin q) r.

Sometimes it is convenient to group sin q with r into the lever arm l, sometimes it is convenient to group sin q with F into the perpendicular force component Fperp, and sometimes it is convenient to simply multiply the three factors F, sin q, and r. Here, we will do the latter.
