Calculating a Torque

Lever Arm Method

Preparation: Torque/Lever Arm/Explain It/Qualitative.

The torque t relative to point O due to a given force is equal to

t = (sign) Fl ,

where F is the magnitude of the force, l (read: el) the lever arm, and the sign is '+' if the torque would cause a counter-clockwise rotation about an axis perpendicular to the screen through O. The torque is '-' if this rotation is clockwise.

Select SHOW LEVER ARM to have the program display the lever arm.
