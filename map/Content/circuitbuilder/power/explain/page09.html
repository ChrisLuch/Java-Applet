Power in Electric Circuits

Answer. There is more power dissipated in the top circuit, 1.5 times as much as in the bottom circuit.

Explanation. If V0 denotes the battery emf, then the power dissipated in the top circuit is

Ptop = V02/R

and in the bottom circuit it is

Pbottom = V02/Rbot.

We need to know the total resistance of the combination of three resistors in the bottom circuit. We have the horizontal resistor in series with the two in-parallel vertical resistors. The latter have a combined resistance equal to R/2. Thus the circuit contains a resistance R and a resistance R/2 in series, which must be added and then give a total resistance Rbot = 1.5 R.

This is consistent with the readings of the two ammeters. The current in the top circuit is 6 A. When the resistance is increased from R to 1.5 R, the current is decreased to 6/1.5 = 4 A.
