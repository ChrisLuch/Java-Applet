Several Batteries

Let the branches be numbered 1 to 3 from left to right, like the resistors. The positive directions in the branches are indicated in the simulation.

Current law at junction A:

I1 + I2 + I3 = 0 .

Voltage law, left loop:

-4I1 + 2.0 + 2I2 - 6.0 = 0 .

Voltage law, right loop:

6.0 - 2I2 + 6I3 + 4.0 = 0 .
