The Wheatstone Bridge

The solution for current I5 can be written as follows. We will use the abbreviation

D = R1R2(R3 + R4) + R3R4(R1 + R2) + R5(R1 + R2)(R3 + R4).
Then
I5 = V0 (R2R3 - R1R4)/D .

For the present values of resistances and battery voltage this gives the value

I5 = -0.133 A .

By inserting an ammeter into the 'cross branch' you can verify this value. The minus sign means that the current is from D to C, as shown by the animation.
