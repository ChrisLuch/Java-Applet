The correct way to connect a voltmeter for measuring the voltage across the resistor is shown on the left.

If you click on the Select Tool and then on the voltmeter, information related to the meter will be displayed.  Change the internal resistance of the meter and click the Calculator icon to implement the change.  Observe how the internal resistance of the voltmeter affects the other readings.  You will need to choose a fairly low internal resistance to see a significant effect.

Switches

Now insert a switch into the circuit that will interrupt the current in the circuit.  To do so, select the Switch Tool  and then, in the Information Area below the circuit diagram, select the Open Switch option by clicking the radio button.  Then click on the wire segment into which you want to insert the switch.  This will replace that segment by an open switch.  When you have done this correctly, the ammeter should display 0 A and the voltmeter 0 V.

To replace the switch with a closed one, deselect the Open Switch option by clicking on it a second time and then click on the switch again. (When the Switch Tool is selected, clicking on any circuit element will insert a switch in its place; in this case the open switch will be replaced by a closed one.)
