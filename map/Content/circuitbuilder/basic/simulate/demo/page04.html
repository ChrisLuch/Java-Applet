Data Entry

The resistance of the resistor is currently set to 10 W.  You can change this value, or insert other resistors into the circuit, as follows.

The data entry field below the circuit diagram shows the resistance value in scientific notation, with three significant digits.  Click anywhere in this field.  Once you have the cursor in the entry field, use either the left or right arrows on the keyboard to move the cursor to where you want it.  Place the cursor just to the left of the digit you want to change.  Change the digit by typing a new value on the keyboard or by using the up and down scroll arrows next to the entry field.

Then, if you click on some segment in the circuit, e.g., the 10-W resistor, a resistor will be inserted there with the resistance value from the data entry field.
