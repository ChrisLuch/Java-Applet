Think About It. The circuit contains three resistors, each of which has a resistance of 2 W. The resistance of the entire combination is, however, not 3x2 = 6 W, but only 3 W. Resistances, in general, don't simply add.

Resistance of the two in-parallel resistors:

1/Rpar = 1/R + 1/R = 2/R .

Thus, Rpar = R/2.
