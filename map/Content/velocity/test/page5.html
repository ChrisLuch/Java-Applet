Vector Addition
Parallelogram Method

The sum of the two vectors, in green, points from the common tail ends of the two vectors to the opposite corner of the parallelogram.

The sum of two vectors is sometimes referred to as their resultant.

Suppose the two vectors are forces. Then the resultant (sum) of the two forces is a single force that has the same effect as the two forces acting simultaneously.
