Velocity

Lesson To Be Learned

An object's velocity, the car's in our simulation, is a vector whose direction at any given instant is the direction in which the object is moving at that instant.

The magnitude of an object's velociy is a scalar equal to the object's speed, indicating how fast the object is moving at a given instant.

Every car has a speedometer to show the magnitude of the car's velocity. It is not so common to find a compass in a car to indicate the direction of the car's velocity. Do you think that cars should have a velocity dial like here that combines both the functions of a speedometer and a compass? Of course, such a dial could only serve as an indicator of the car's velocity, not as a controller of the velocity.
