Scalar Components of Vectors

Solution:To calculate the scalar components of a vector, you need to know the trigonometric sine and cosine functions. See Trig Functions under Get Information.

ax = a cos q
= 5 cos 127o = -3.0

ay = a sin q
= 5 sin 127o = 4.0

These equations are true for vectors pointing into any quadrant.

You can practise calculating components corresponding to different axes (drag the tip of either axis) or for different vectors (drag the tip of the vector). The results will always be displayed so that you can check your calculations.
