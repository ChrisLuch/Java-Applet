Scalar Components of Vectors

The diagram shows the scalar components of a vector a, the x-component in green and the y-component in yellow.

The scalar components of vector a are scalars, denoted ax and ay.

If the tail end of the vector is at the origin, its scalar components are simply the x and y coordinates of the tip of the vector, here equal to -4 and 6, respectively.

You can denote vector a in terms of its scalar components as follows:

a = (ax, ay) = (-4, 6).
