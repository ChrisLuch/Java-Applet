Scalar Components of Vectors

Scalar components make calculating with vectors very easy. E.g., one adds or subtracts two vectors simply by adding or subtracting their components.

Thus, if

= (-6.4, 5.0)

and

= (2.6, 3.0),

then

+

= (-6.4 + 2.6, 5.0 + 3.0)

= (-3.8, 8.0)

and

-

= (-6.4 - 2.6, 5.0 - 3.0)

= (-9.0, 2.0).
