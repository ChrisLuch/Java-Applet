What is the Acceleration?

Dv2 = -2 [ (m1/M) g Dh1 + (m1/M) g Dh1 ].

Our goal is to get an expression for the acceleration. For motion along a straight line, Dv2 is related to the acceleration component along the line by

Dv2 = 2 az Dz .

This equation deals with the components of the displacement and acceleration vectors along the line of motion, called z-components here. The orientation of the z-axis must be defined. The choice made here is indicated in the diagram.

This equation involving az holds only for constant acceleration, which is what we have here.
