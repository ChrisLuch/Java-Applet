What is the Acceleration?

Did you notice that for some settings the left block accelerates up the incline, for others down the incline? If not, you may want to experiment with the system some more. Also, can you find settings for which the acceleration is zero?

Since the acceleration can be in two directions, we need to define axes for the inclines (see the z-axes in the diagram) and work with the acceleration component az. The way the two z-axes are chosen, az will have the same sign for both blocks.
