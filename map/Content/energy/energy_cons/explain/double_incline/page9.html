What is the Acceleration?

We have now derived the following expression for az:

az = -[ (m1/M) sin a - (m2/M) sin b ] g.

Substituting the given values gives, since M = 1 kg,

az = -[0.7 sin 30o - 0.3 sin 60o]x9.8

= -0.88 m s-2.

This is the value observed earlier. The minus sign means that the acceleration is in the negative z-direction, i.e., that the left block accelerates down the incline.

You can now observe the acceleration for other settings of the system, and check that the equation for az agrees with the observed values.
