What is the Acceleration?

With m1 = 700 g, m2 = 300 g, a = 30o, and b = 60o, the acceleration of the blocks has the magnitude 0.88 m s-2.

Now click START. You will not be able to stop the motion this time. It will stop automatically, at t = 1.5 s and v = 1.33 m/s.

Thus, the acceleration has the magnitude

a = Dv/Dt = (v - 0)/(t - 0)

= v/t = 1.33/1.5 = 0.88 m s-2.

Your time and speed readings may be slightly different because of round-off error.
