Potential Energy Quantitative

Answer. The work-kinetic energy theorem implies that the work Wgrav done on the ball during a given time interval by the force of gravity, Fgrav, is equal to the change in the ball's kinetic energy,

Wgrav = DKE.

We want to find PE so that

DPE = - DKE.

Therefore, we can take

DPE = - Wgrav.

The last equation is the general definition of the change in gravitational potential energy.

Write down an expression for Wgrav in terms of Dy, and then obtain an expression for PE. When done, go on to Page 8 to compare.
