The Concept of Potential Energy

What can we use for PE that depends on the positions of the particles and that added to the total kinetic energy KE = KE1 + KE2 gives a constant,

KE + PE = const?

To find this PE note that this equation implies that the changes Delta PE amd Delta KE that occur in a given time interval must add up to zero,

Delta KE + Delta PE = 0.

The change in kinetic energy of the system, Delta KE, is equal to the work W done by the gravitational forces exerted by the two particles on each other, according to the work-kinetic energy theorem.

Substituting W for Delta KE, the last equation gives

Delta PE = -W.

Note that W depends on the initial and final positions of the particles.
