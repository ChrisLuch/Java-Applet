The Concept of Potential Energy

Suppose it is possible to find a potential energy (PE) for the system such that

E = PE + KE1 + KE2 = constant.

Then PE must decrease as KE = KE1 + KE2 increases. Thus PE must behave as in the simulation on the left. Run the simulation and observe how the length of the blue potential energy bar changes as the kinetic energy changes.
