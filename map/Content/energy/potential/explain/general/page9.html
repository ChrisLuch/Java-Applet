The Concept of Potential Energy

Summary so far. The definition

DPE = -W,

defines the change in potential energy of the two-particle system during a given time interval. W is the work done during this time interval by the gravitational forces that the particles are exerting on each other.

By the work-kinetic energy theorem, this definition of DPE ensures that there is no change in the mechanical energy E = KE + PE of the system, because

DE = DKE + DPE = DKE - W = 0.

The definition above defines the change DPE in potential energy. What about the potential energy itself? This will be explained on the next page.
