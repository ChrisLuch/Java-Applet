Energy

Watch the video from the Video Encyclopedia of Physics.

It demonstrates several aspects of energy:

1. There are different forms of energy:

  • chemical energy in the young lady's muscles,
  • rotational energy (which is a kind of mechanical energy) in the heavy weights,
  • electrical energy in the generator,
  • light energy coming from the bulb,
  • thermal energy in the bulb and elsewhere,

2. Energy can transform from one kind to another. Here it is being transformed in the order listed above.

3. Energy can be transferred from one place to another, e.g., from the young lady to the heavy weights or from the interior of the bulb to the exterior.

4. The total energy, the sum of all individual energies, is conserved while energy is being transformed and transferred.
