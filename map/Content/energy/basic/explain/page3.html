Energy Simulation

In the applet window on the left, there is a white area containing red and blue blocks. This represents a system containing two kinds of energy:

  • kinetic energy (KE)
  • potential energy (PE).

The energy E, whose nature the applet does not specify, outside of the system is represented by green blocks.

By double-clicking an energy block (1 joule) inside the system you can change it from one kind into the other kind. You can also drag energy blocks out of the system or into the system.

Observe that whatever you do, the sum of all the energies, inside and outside the system, is conserved. It always is equal to 46 joules (J).

For details on how the applet functions, consult Help and ShowMe under Applet Help on the applet's Help menu. There is also a Lesson related to the applet under Applet Help.
