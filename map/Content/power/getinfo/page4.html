Three-dimensional motion

The analogous expression for the power transferred in three dimensions is

P = Fxvx + Fyvy + Fyvz = Fv cos q.

Comment. These expressions for mechanical power transfer also apply to systems of particles or extended objects. Such systems or objects will have forces acting on them at specific points. To calculate the power transfer due to each of these forces from the expressions above, one must take the velocity of the point at which the force is acting.
