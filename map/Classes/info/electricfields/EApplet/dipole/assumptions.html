Assumptions - Electric Field Due To Two Point Charges

The applet simulates the electric field and the equipotential lines (Curves) accompanying two electric point charges.

The applet makes the following assumptions:

  • The two source objects, i.e., the objects responsible for the existence of the electric field, are perfect spheres of non-zero radius..
  • The charge densities of the source objects are uniform throughout the object.

A consequence of these assumptions is that, for points outside the source objects, the source objects behave like point charges.

The applet does not permit field points inside the finite source objects. Its purpose is to simulate the electric field due to a pair of point charges.
